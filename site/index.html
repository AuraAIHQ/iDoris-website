On the name · a small, unglamorous story

AuraAI was the AI's suggestion — a name you've seen a thousand times. By the time we went to register the domain, someone had taken it years ago.

So the main domain became iDoris.ai. Not a compromise — an improvement. iDoris is both what the org is called and what the core product is: the organizational brain. The work should be named after the brain anyway.

01 / Three Directions

Workbench builds tools, iDoris trains models,
Hyphae connects reality and executes tasks

These aren't three parallel product lines. They're three preconditions for the same thing. Drop any one and the brain is just a demo.

DIRECTION 01

Self-FDE-WorkBenchOpen

Sharpen the tool before the work

An open workbench — built for ourselves, and for every other builder. Forge your own AI weapon.

FDE — Forward Deployed Engineer — is Palantir's coinage for institutionalized grunt work: you have to get inside the customer to see the real jam. We think the role outlasts the name. It's the ANE, AI Native Engineer: not deployed, but present.

The orgs that can't afford an FDE — can they FDE themselves? We think so.

The brain that actually fits

A frontier model knows everything except your thing. iDoris tunes the model small enough, specific enough, close-fitting enough — until every individual, org, company, and eventually city has a brain of its own.

DIRECTION 03

HyphaeIn progress

The brain's hands and feet

A brain needs limbs. We use AI's different capabilities and different workflows to raise how well humans work and coordinate — a legion of agents under the brain's command.

  • Agent accounts & security — identity, authorization, blast radius
  • Cross-org agent protocols — when every org has agents, how do they talk?
02 / Thesis

The future leads the present

If AI pushes the cost of productivity down toward the price of electricity, everyone has to answer one question: when my job is taken by AI, what's left for me to do?

What gets restructured is the work that exists purely in pursuit of extreme efficiency. The era needs people who collaborate with AI better, and it needs AI Native Engineers.

— on what keeps its value

We believe three roles will emerge, and have already started emerging. They will continue to hold core value.

Role Why it survives What we provide
🗣 Expresser AI will never say "this restaurant is pretty good" — because it isn't a person One-click multi-platform publishing · illustration · de-AI-ing the prose
💡 Innovator When the whole map is fog, someone walks a path through it Pain-point matching · experiment subjects · coordination
🔧 Builder = ANE The "small problem" you fix is what someone else endures every day Model selection · LoRA · memory/context · agents

The goal: build an open-source AI organization that contributes more digital public goods to humanity's digital future — and by doing so, raises the welfare of digital life itself.
