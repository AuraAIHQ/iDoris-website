# Doris Character Lock

This repository uses Doris as the fixed mascot for future website illustrations.

## Source of Truth

Use `docs/doris-character-reference.png` as the primary visual reference. Do not reinterpret Doris from memory or redesign her unless explicitly requested.

## Fixed Appearance

- Friendly young female AI assistant with an approachable, clever, calm presence.
- Black hair in the same high ponytail silhouette shown in the reference, with the orange tie and loose hand-drawn strands preserved.
- Black puffer jacket over a white hoodie.
- Visible `iDORIS` chest patch with a small red lowercase `i` and white `DORIS`, plus a small `AI` sleeve patch when the pose allows.
- Black trousers with small orange accents.
- Clean white low-top sneakers with black pixel-comic outlines.
- Pixel-like, independent comic linework: loose black ink, dry-brush texture, small hatch marks, flat limited colors.
- Palette: black, charcoal, off-white, white, orange accents, and restrained cyan data elements.

## Illustration Rules

- Doris should replace the previous 小J figure in homepage and brand illustrations.
- Preserve the scene's core meaning when replacing older art. For example: installing an organizational brain, handing over an AI tool, tailoring a brain, or conducting an agent team.
- Keep Doris human and friendly, not robotic, armored, glamorous, or childish.
- Avoid hats, headphones, backpacks, weapons, heavy makeup, long redesigns, or alternate outfits unless the user explicitly asks.
- Do not add text inside generated illustration assets unless the design specifically requires it.

## Homepage Asset Prompts

When regenerating homepage art, use the reference image for Doris identity and the current asset only for scene composition. The old figure's face, hair, body, clothing, and accessories must not be copied.
